3D Printing at IEEE RAS PESU

IEEE RAS PESU now has Creality Ender 3 S1 and Creality CR10 V3 3D Printers! Filament used is PLA filaments.

The following are the terms and conditions:

  • Slot will be alloted based on the number of printing hours.
  • Maximum printing hours is 24 hours.
  • Filament limit is 100g per model.
  • The file format accepted is STL. For multiple files, send an archive of STL file.
