Random Robotics

Where Robotic Fantasies Come True

The process is fairly simple where the students shall participate individually or as a team and submit their ideas for that particular theme. The core team of IEEE RAS PESU shall review all these ideas and select the two most innovative and viable ones. This is followed by the mentoring process for the selected students who proceed to bring their ideas into life!!
