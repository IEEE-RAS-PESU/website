ROBOFEST 2.0

  • 21st October 2022

    Inauguration of ROBOFEST 2.0

    Performance by Encore, the Western Dance Group, followed by a speech by Dr. V Krishna, Dean of Student Affairs, unfurling of the banner using drones built by the IEEE RAS PESU.
  • 27th October 2022

    Start of Registrations & Project Roadshow

    Registrations open on the website. Major projects guided by the club and other interesting robotics projects will be showcased for the entire student body.
  • 14th November 2022

    Club Orientation & End Of Registrations

    An informative session to talk about the achievements and progress of the official robotics club of the university as well as how to join.
  • 15-16th November 2022

    Online Workshop

    All registered teams attend the online workshops on Basics of Raspberry Pi, Image Processing, IoT(Internet of Things), ROS for beginners taught by experienced student mentors and receive certificates.
