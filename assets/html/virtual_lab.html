About Virtual Lab

The Virtual Lab

Introduction

Do you want to learn about Arduino or Implement ROS on Robotics without purchasing the hardware, then Virtual Lab is the platform for you!

It is a one of a kind of a lab where in anyone from anywhere in the world can book a slot to our platform and use our robot on a setup for 1 hour where a person can work on the robot in the following areas such as:

  • Basics of Arduino
  • Raspberri PI
  • Implementing ROS on the robot
  • Learning OpenCV and motor drivers

Benefits of Virtual Lab

Click Robotics

Enabling the access to an Autonomous Mobile Robot on a click of a button.

Work Robotics

1 hour slot for free to work on robots from anywhere in the world.

Learn Robotics

Learn the basics of robotics and implement them here through our curated playlist.

Implement Robotics

Learn Implement your algorithms, ROS libraries and much more on our lab.

Current Progress

Our team is currently working on the deployment of the backend service for hobbyists and researchers to access our lab with a latency of less than 10ms. Previously, we did the same using AnyDesk with a latency of over 200ms. The design and setup of the lab is up and running.

The slot bookings are scheduled to start from the first quarter of 2023. Stay tuned!
