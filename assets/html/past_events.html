  • PAST EVENTS
  • July 6th,2021Project Oasis was a month long event of fun, packed with loads of learning and innovation! The speed and wit of our participants were tested and they were given the liberty to work on our real robot from the comfort of their home virtually.
  • Jan 29th, 2021 We organized a virtual workshop on Basics Of Robot Operating System(ROS).
  • Jan 27th, 2021 We conducted a virtual workshop on Hardware Integration with Image Processing.
  • Jan 25th, 2021 We conducted a hands-on Image Processing Workshop using Python for freshers.
  • Jan 21st, 2021 We organized a virtual workshop on Basics Of Arduino for freshers.
  • May 29th, 2020 We organized a webinar on AI and Robotics with Mr. Sreekanth Pallavoor, which gave an insight of AI and Robotic Applications in Industries.
  • May 22nd, 2020 We conducted a webinar on Predictive Maintainence of Large Industrial Equipments using IoT System by Dr. Kumar Padmanabh who is a Senior Scientist in EBTIC
  • May 18th-29th, 2020 We conducted our second webinar series on Practical Mathematics for Robotic Applications.
  • May 11th-15th, 2020 We conducted a webinar series on Mathematical Intuition for Robotics which covered various topics like Kinematics, Trajectories, Force and Torque, and Microcontrollers.
  • May 7th, 2020 We organized a talk on IEEE Standards On Robotics by Mr. Srikanth Chandrasekaran who is a Senior Director of Standards And Technology IEEE, India.
  • Apr 24th, 2020 We conducted an Ideathon where students came up with ideas for different real world problems that can be solved using robotics and automation.
  • Nov 16th,2019Introduction to Automation - by Debayan Das. The third and final workshop held in the series. The students were introduced to the field of automation in robotics and a bumper crowd ensured that the content delivered was thoroughly absorbed.
  • Nov 13th,2019 Voice Controlled Robots - by Sanjayitha. The workshop had a crowd of 80 students. The students learnt how to build 2 different skills and were thoroughly enthralled with the platform and the skills they built.
  • Oct 23rd,2019Convolution Neural Network - by Aravind and Manasa. The Workshop took place in the CSE Seminar Hall, and a crowd of 80 students attended it. The students were taught using the Google Colab platform and were tasked with building their own Neural Network.
